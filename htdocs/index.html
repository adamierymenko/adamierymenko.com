Adam Ierymenko's Personal Site

This is the personal web site of Adam Ierymenko, developer and founder of ZeroTier, Inc. I live in Southern California with my wife and two daughters. I am best known for network protocols today, evolutionary computation stuff in an earlier life, and random juvenile hacks in a still earlier one. Here you'll find projects, writings, and anything else I find interesting.

The site's style is an ode to my childhood when I called BBSes on a 9600 baud modem and learned to program on Commodore and IBM PC/AT computers. I spent a lot of time using applications that ran on MS-DOS and looked a lot like this.

Needless to say the things here do not necessarily represent the "official" positions of my company blah blah blah.
